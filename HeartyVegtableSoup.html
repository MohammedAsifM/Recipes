• Hearty Vegetable Soup

    Ingredients

    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 large onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 3 carrots, peeled and chopped
    • 2 celery stalks, chopped
    • 1 zucchini, chopped
    • 1 yellow squash, chopped
    • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
    • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and chopped
    • 1 (28-ounce) can diced tomatoes
    • 6 cups vegetable broth
    • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
    • Salt and pepper, to taste
    • 1 cup frozen peas
    • 2 cups chopped kale or spinach
    • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (optional)
    • Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
    2. Add the minced garlic and cook for another minute, until fragrant.
    3. Stir in the carrots, celery, zucchini, yellow squash, red bell pepper, and green beans. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, until the vegetables begin to soften.
    4. Add the diced tomatoes (with their juices), vegetable broth, dried thyme, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil.
    5. Reduce the heat to low and let the soup simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
    6. Stir in the frozen peas and chopped kale or spinach. Cook for another 5 minutes, until the greens are wilted and tender.
    7. Taste and adjust the seasoning with more salt and pepper if needed.
    8. Serve the soup hot, garnished with chopped fresh parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
    9. Enjoy your hearty vegetable soup!
